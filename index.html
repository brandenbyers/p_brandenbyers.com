branden byers: in the weeds on running, vegan nutrition, and data

Software engineer and Masters runner pondering performance, longevity, and vegan nutrition. Expect tangents on training, methods, and coffee.

I started running at 42 without feeling like I knew how to run. I’ve been vegan since 2017. Looking back, I spent most of my life not understanding how to eat. I think I’m finally starting to learn now.

I’m the kind of person who reads manuals cover to cover. My body didn’t come with one, so I’m writing it myself; one experiment at a time. Sometimes the experiments work. Sometimes they don’t. I share both.
